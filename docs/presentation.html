The Problem

You know your balance.
But can you plan ahead?

Most timecard systems tell you: "You have 47.3 hours today."

But they can't answer:

  • ❓ "If I take a week off in September, will I have enough hours?"
  • ❓ "What will my balance be on December 31?"
  • ❓ "Am I going to lose hours to the carryover cap?"
  • ❓ "Can I afford a sick day without ruining my vacation plans?"

Calendar Features

  •  Click any work day to plan time off
  •  Holidays shown with themed emoji
  •  Paydays marked with 💰
  • 📊 Projected balance on every future day
  • ⏰ Partial days show time range (e.g. 11a – 2p)
  • 🔴 Unaffordable days highlighted in red
  • 🎉 Fun tooltips — "Long weekend!" on Fridays
  • ⌨️ Arrow keys to navigate months
Day Planner

Smart Features

Built to think ahead for you

🏦

Bank Hours

Log extra hours worked beyond your regular day. Bank hours are used first for time off and get paid out during the Dec-Feb window.

💡

Smart Insights

"You can take 2 sick days and still cover your plans." Data-driven insights that help you make confident decisions about time off.

📊

Projection Engine

Day-by-day balance projection accounting for accruals, tier transitions, planned time off, holidays, and carryover caps.

⚙️

Fully Customizable

Accrual tiers, pay periods, holidays, work schedule, carryover policies, bank hours payout windows — match any employer's rules.
